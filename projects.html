These are some projects I've worked on. This page is really outdated!


Feb. 2017 | Mood for Thought: Mood for Thought is a web app built in js that allows users to receive 'mystery' recommendations from other users for a certain mood, and to recommend tidbits and media pertaining to a mood to other people around the world.

Feb. 2016 | UC 4th Annual Case Competition: I competed in a case competition in the first week of January featuring several schools around California, sponsored by Kaiser Permanente. You can read more about my experience here.

Jan. 2016 | A stream overlay: League of Legends at UCLA started streaming tournaments with twitch.tv. I built a stream overlay to have a clean interface, as well as to support any sponsors.

Jan. 2016 | International League Tournament: We ran a tournament in collaboration with UCLA's International Student Society in February and March of 2016. The tournament was livestreamed on Afreeca TV, and had teams of players representing several NGOs to raise awareness. The winning team represented Charity: Water.

Dec. 2015 | This website: I'm working on a personal website, through github pages, that will showcase things that are important to me.
